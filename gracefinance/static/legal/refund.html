Refund & Dispute Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026 · Last Updated: March 9, 2026

1. Digital Subscription Service

GraceFinance is a digital subscription service. No physical goods are shipped. When you subscribe, you gain immediate access to the features associated with your tier.

2. Automatic Renewal

All paid subscriptions (GracePro and GracePremium) renew automatically unless canceled before the renewal date. You may cancel at any time through your account settings or the Stripe Customer Portal.

3. General Refund Policy

Because GraceFinance provides immediate access to digital services upon subscription, all sales are generally final.

4. Discretionary Refund Consideration

GraceFinance may consider refund requests for:

  • Duplicate charges due to technical error
  • Billing error — charged for an unauthorized tier
  • Extended service unavailability due to an issue on our end
  • First-time subscription within 48 hours of purchase

Refund requests must be submitted to support@gracefinance.co within 14 days of the charge.

5. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed through Stripe and returned to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

6. Downgrades and Cancellations

Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. Cancellations retain access until the end of the current period. No partial refunds are issued.

7. Annual Subscriptions

Annual subscriptions are billed as a single discounted payment. Partial refunds for unused months are not provided.

8. Account Suspension

Accounts suspended for Terms of Service violations are not entitled to refunds.

9. Chargebacks

Please contact us before initiating a dispute with your bank. We reserve the right to provide documentation to contest chargebacks. Accounts with unresolved chargebacks may be suspended.

10. Free Tier

The Free tier does not involve payment and is not subject to this Refund Policy.

11. Changes to This Policy

Material changes will be communicated via email or notice within the Service.
